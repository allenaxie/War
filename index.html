Time for WAR! Play 4 cards.
How to play

Setup

Each player receives 26 cards into their deck.

Gameplay

Play the next card in your deck. If you'd like, you may shuffle your deck once per round.

Each round, the player with the higher ranking card in their hand wins the round. Winner of each round takes all the cards from both players' hands and into their deck. If both players have a card of same rank in their hand, go to WAR!

WAR

Both players must play 4 more cards consecutively. 3 of those cards will be placed face down into the "pile" and the 4th card face up into their hand.

The player with the higher ranking card in their hand takes all the cards from each players' hand and pile. If the 4th card is of same rank, repeat WAR until one side draws a higher card.

Goal

Win all 52 cards before your opponent!
