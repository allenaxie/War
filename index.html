Round: 1
Time for WAR! Play 4 cards.
How to play

Setup

Each player receives 26 cards into their deck.

Gameplay

Play the next card in your deck. If you'd like you may shuffle your deck once per turn.

Each round, the player with the higher ranking card in their hand wins the round. Winner of each round takes all the cards from both players' hands into their deck. If both player has the same ranking card in their hand, go to WAR!

WAR

Both players must play 4 more cards consecutively. 3 of those cards face down into the "pile" and the 4th card face up into their hand.

Player with the higher ranking card in their hand takes all the cards from each players' hand and pile. If 4th card is of same rank, repeat WAR until one side wins the round.

Goal

Win all 52 cards before your opponent!
